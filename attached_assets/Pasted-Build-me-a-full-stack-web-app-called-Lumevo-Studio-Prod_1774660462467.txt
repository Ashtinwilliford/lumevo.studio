Build me a full-stack web app called Lumevo Studio.

## Product Concept
Lumevo is an AI social media manager. The core difference is:

You upload. Lumevo learns. We create.

Users upload content they already make, and the app learns their:
- voice
- style
- personality
- audience preferences
- visual vibe
- fonts
- pacing
- content structure

The more they upload and create, the better the app gets at generating content that feels like them.

## Core Goal
This should not feel like a generic AI tool.
It should feel like a personalized AI social media manager that improves over time and eventually helps run the user’s content system.
